sm_thornthwaite_mather Module Reference

Performs the actual soil-moisture balance once precip, snowmelt, runoff, and ET have been calculated. Soil moisture for a given soil accumulated potential water loss (APWL) is determined by means of Thornthwaite and Mathers' (1957) tables.
